About ViMovies

ViMovies is a free movie and TV streaming app built for Android TV, Google TV, Fire TV, and Android mobile devices. Our mission is simple: a premium, TV-first viewing experience should not require a subscription.

What We Build

We focus on the details that make living-room viewing pleasant: remote-first navigation, fast startup on modest TV hardware, 4K playback, automatic subtitles, and offline downloads. The app is updated regularly with performance and feature improvements.

Editorial Standards

The guides and reviews on our blog are written by the ViMovies team and based on hands-on testing with real devices. When we compare ViMovies to other apps, we test them side by side on identical hardware and disclose our methodology.

Questions, feedback, or press inquiries? Reach us through our contact page. For copyright matters, see our DMCA policy.
