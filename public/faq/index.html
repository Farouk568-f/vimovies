Before You Start

New to installing apps outside an app store? It's a two-minute process called sideloading, fully supported by Android. The short version: download the APK, open it, allow the one-time permission, install. Device-specific instructions live on the Android TV, Google TV, and Firestick pages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ViMovies completely free?

Yes. There is no subscription, trial, or hidden fee. The app is free to download and free to use.

Do I need to create an account?

No. ViMovies works without any sign-up. Your watchlist and progress are stored on your device.

Which devices does ViMovies support?

Android TV, Google TV (including Chromecast with Google TV), Fire TV Stick, and Android phones and tablets running Android 7.0 or newer.

Why isn't ViMovies on the Play Store?

Independent streaming apps are distributed directly as APK files. Installing them is a standard Android capability called sideloading.

Is sideloading ViMovies safe?

Installing the APK from the official site is safe. We publish every release ourselves. Avoid third-party mirror sites — see our full safety analysis for details.

How do I update the app?

Download the newest APK from the official download page and install it over the current version. Settings and watch history carry over.

Can I watch offline?

Yes. Movies and episodes can be downloaded inside the app and played without a connection.

Does ViMovies work outside the US?

Yes, the app works internationally. Content availability can vary slightly by region.

The app buffers constantly. What can I do?

Switch to 5 GHz Wi-Fi or Ethernet, lower the playback quality one notch, and restart the device. Our troubleshooting guide lists every fix in order of effectiveness.

How do I uninstall ViMovies?

Like any app: long-press the icon on your TV launcher and choose Uninstall, or use Settings → Apps.
