How to Install ViMovies: Complete Step-by-Step Guide

By the ViMovies Team · Updated July 6, 2026

ViMovies is a free movie and TV app built for the living room. This guide walks you through installing it on Android TV, Google TV, Fire TV Stick, and Android phones or tablets — with clear steps, screenshots-level detail, and fixes for the most common installation errors.

What You Need Before Installing

  • A device running Android 7.0 or newer (Android TV 9+ recommended)
  • About 60 MB of free storage
  • A stable internet connection
  • 5 minutes of your time
Safety first: only download ViMovies from the official website. Third-party APK mirrors may bundle modified or unsafe versions.

How to Install ViMovies on Android TV & Google TV

Step 1: Allow installation from your browser or file manager

Open Settings → Apps → Security & Restrictions → Unknown Sources (on Google TV: Settings → Privacy → Security → Install unknown apps) and enable the toggle for the app you will use to download the APK.

Step 2: Download the APK

Open the official ViMovies website on your TV browser, or use the Downloader app (free on the Play Store) and enter the download URL shown on the homepage.

Step 3: Install

When the download finishes, select Install. The process takes under 30 seconds on most TVs.

Step 4: Launch and enjoy

Open ViMovies from your apps row. The TV interface is designed for remote-control (D-pad) navigation, so no mouse or keyboard is needed.

How to Install ViMovies on Android Phones & Tablets

  1. Visit the official ViMovies website in Chrome.
  2. Tap the Download for Mobile button.
  3. If prompted, allow Chrome to install unknown apps (Settings → Apps → Chrome → Install unknown apps).
  4. Open the downloaded APK and tap Install.

How to Install ViMovies on Fire TV Stick

  1. From the Fire TV home screen, search for and install the Downloader app.
  2. Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options and enable Install unknown apps for Downloader.
  3. Open Downloader, enter the official ViMovies download URL, and press Go.
  4. Install the APK when prompted, then delete the installer file to free up space.

Troubleshooting Common Installation Problems

“App not installed” error

This usually means a corrupted download or an older conflicting version. Delete the APK, uninstall any previous version of ViMovies, and download a fresh copy.

“Blocked by Play Protect”

Play Protect flags any app installed outside the Play Store. Tap More details → Install anyway. This is standard for all sideloaded apps.

Download button does nothing on TV browser

Some TV browsers block direct downloads. Use the Downloader app instead — it is the most reliable method on both Android TV and Fire TV.

Not enough storage

Clear the cache of unused apps under Settings → Apps, or uninstall apps you no longer use. ViMovies itself needs only about 60 MB.

After Installation: First-Time Setup Tips

  • Pick your subtitle language in Settings for automatic subtitle loading.
  • Enable Continue Watching so ViMovies remembers your position across sessions.
  • Add favorites to build a personal watchlist accessible from the home row.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ViMovies free to install?

Yes. ViMovies is completely free to download and install. There are no subscription fees, sign-up requirements, or hidden charges.

Do I need to enable unknown sources?

On Android TV and mobile, apps installed outside the Play Store require the “Install unknown apps” permission for your file manager or browser. You can disable it again after installation.

Does ViMovies work on Fire TV Stick?

Yes. Fire TV runs Fire OS, which is based on Android, so the same APK installs through the Downloader app.

How do I update ViMovies?

Download the latest APK from the official site and install it over the existing app. Your settings and watch history are preserved.
