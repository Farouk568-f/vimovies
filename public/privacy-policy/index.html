Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ViMovies (“we”, “our”) handles information when you use our website and application.

Information We Collect

  • Website: standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) used for security and performance monitoring.
  • Application: the app does not require account creation. Locally stored preferences (watch history, favorites, subtitle language) remain on your device.

How We Use Information

We use aggregate, non-identifying data to improve app performance, fix bugs, and understand which features are used. We do not sell personal information.

Third-Party Services

The website may use analytics services that set cookies. You can block cookies in your browser without losing access to any content.

Data Retention & Your Rights

Server logs are retained for a limited period for security purposes. You may request information about, or deletion of, data associated with you by contacting us via the contact page.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the date above.
