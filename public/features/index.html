ViMovies Features: Full Breakdown

ViMovies is a free entertainment app for Android TV, Google TV, Fire TV, and Android devices. Here's a complete, honest breakdown of what it does — and a few things it doesn't do yet.

Download ViMovies Free

Streaming

  • Movies, TV series, and anime in one app, organized by genre, popularity, and release date.
  • HD playback with adaptive quality that steps down gracefully on slower connections.
  • Fast source loading — most titles start playing in a few seconds.
  • Subtitles for the majority of content, toggled from the player.

Downloads & Offline Viewing

  • Save movies and individual episodes for offline playback.
  • Choose the download location — internal storage or USB on TV devices.
  • Manage and delete downloads from a single screen.

Built for the Living Room

  • Remote-first design: every element reachable with a D-pad, with clear focus highlighting.
  • Lightweight: roughly 60 MB, smooth on entry-level boxes with 1.5 GB RAM.
  • Instant resume: watch progress is stored per episode, no account needed.

Search & Discovery

  • Fast title search with on-screen keyboard support.
  • Curated rows: trending, new releases, top-rated, and per-genre rails.
  • Personal watchlist stored on-device.

What ViMovies Doesn't Do (Yet)

Honesty matters more than a long feature list. As of the current release there is no multi-device sync (progress is per-device), no user profiles, and the iOS version is not available. Follow the changelog to see what lands in each update.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does ViMovies have subtitles?

Yes. Subtitles are available for most titles and can be toggled from the playback screen.

Can I create a watchlist?

Yes. Add any title to your list from its detail page; the list syncs locally on your device.

Does it track what I've watched?

Watch progress is stored on-device, so episodes resume exactly where you stopped — with no account required.

Are there ads?

The app is free and ad-supported in places, but there are no forced video ads interrupting playback.
